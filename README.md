# SmartApply
### AI-Powered Resume Analyzer and Job Tracker  
SmartApply helps job seekers analyze their resumes, improve them with AI suggestions, and manage job applications effortlessly — all in one place.
 
---

## Vision
Most job seekers don’t know how well their resumes perform against real job descriptions or struggle to keep track of applications.  
SmartApply acts as your personal **AI-driven Applicant Tracking System**, analyzing resumes, extracting keywords, suggesting improvements, and helping you manage job listings efficiently.

---

## Features (Planned in Phases)

### Phase 1 – AI Resume Analyzer
- Upload your resume (PDF/DOCX)
- AI extracts key information (skills, education, experience)
- Keyword extraction and relevance score
- Resume score and personalized improvement suggestions

### Phase 2 – Job Listings Integration
- Scrape or fetch job postings using Python
- Filter by role, experience, or keywords
- Save or mark jobs as applied
- Clear outdated/unapplied jobs

### Phase 3 – Email Reminder & OAuth
- Gmail OAuth integration
- Schedule reminder emails to apply for saved jobs
- Email verification for user accounts
